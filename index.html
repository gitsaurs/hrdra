Official Notice & Verification Center

This site is the only official website of Haridra Groups. Beware of fraudsters who claim they are from Haridra Groups. Use the verification center below to confirm official channels.

How to verify

  1. Official domains always use haridragroups.example or approved subdomains.
  2. Official email addresses end with @haridragroups.example.
  3. We will never ask for upfront payments through unverified personal accounts — always use the business invoice sent from the official email.
  4. Call our verified phone numbers listed on this website's Contact page.

Tip: If you receive unexpected contact, cross-check the sender on this page or forward suspicious messages to security@haridragroups.example.
